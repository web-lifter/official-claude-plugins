# Home TLC Calendar — Reference Material

## AU State-by-State Compliance Quick-Reference

**This is general information — always verify with your state's authority for current rules.**

### NSW — Fair Trading + Office of Local Government

- **Smoke alarms:** AS3786 compliant; hardwired in new builds; battery test annual; replace every 10 yrs
- **Pool barrier:** AS1926 compliance; certificate every 3 years (Swimming Pools Register)
- **Termites:** No mandate; AS3660.2 inspection annual recommended in timber/coastal homes
- **Electrical:** RCD recommended; not statutory but heavily encouraged

### VIC — Victorian Building Authority + ESV

- **Smoke alarms:** AS3786; mains-powered in homes built/altered post-1997
- **Gas:** Mandatory CO test biennially for Type-A gas appliances by licensed gas fitter
- **Pool barrier:** Re-inspection cycle every 4 yrs from late 2020
- **Termites:** No mandate; annual inspection recommended

### QLD — Queensland Building and Construction Commission (QBCC)

- **Smoke alarms:** Photoelectric interconnected in every bedroom + connection to hallway by 2027 (already mandatory in new builds + renovations)
- **Pool barrier:** Annual Form 23 certificate from licensed inspector
- **Termites:** Annual inspection strongly recommended in subtropical climate
- **Cyclone (north of latitude 26):** Specific cyclone-rated requirements

### WA — Building Commission + Department of Mines

- **Smoke alarms:** AS3786 hardwired; battery test annual
- **RCDs:** Mandatory in habitable rooms since 2009
- **Pool barrier:** Annual inspection required; cycle varies by LGA

### SA — Office of the Technical Regulator

- **Smoke alarms:** 10-year-life with mains backup in renovated/new builds
- **Pool barrier:** Annual inspection; mandatory before water filled
- **Termites:** No mandate

### TAS — Consumer, Building and Occupational Services

- **Smoke alarms:** AS3786; hardwired in new builds
- **Pool barrier:** Annual inspection required
- **Termites:** Less critical (climate); still recommended

### ACT — Access Canberra

- **Smoke alarms:** Annual test
- **Pool barrier:** Biennial inspection
- **Termites:** Annual inspection recommended

### NT — NT Government

- **Smoke alarms:** AS3786
- **Pool barrier:** Annual inspection
- **Cyclone:** Cyclone shelter / wind-rated windows mandatory north of certain zones
- **Termites:** High humidity → 6-monthly inspection recommended

## Bushfire-Zone Additional Tasks (NSW, VIC, ACT, TAS, SA, WA)

- Annual fuel-load assessment (60+ days before fire season)
- Gutter clean late spring (debris = ember catchment)
- Ember-screen check
- Driveway access width (emergency vehicle)
- Water supply (rainwater tank with petrol pump or fire-service connection)
- Family evacuation plan + meeting point + 2 escape routes
- Bushfire survival kit (radio, water, food, masks, woollens)

## Coastal / Salt-Air Additional Tasks

- Rust check on door + window hardware (6-monthly)
- Anode check on hot-water service (every 3 yrs not 5)
- Outdoor furniture annual wax/seal
- Roof + gutter check after storms
- Salt-air washing of windows + outdoor surfaces seasonally
