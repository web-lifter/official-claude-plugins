## Tasks by Category

### Smoke alarms + safety

- **QLD specific:** Photoelectric smoke alarms required in every bedroom + interconnected (full compliance by 2027 — already mandatory in new + renovated builds)
- Test push-button monthly; replace battery annually (or full unit every 10 yrs)
- CO alarm if any gas appliance — annual test

### Gutters + roof

- Clean gutters May (pre-winter) + October (post-spring leaf fall + pre-storm season)
- Visual roof check at same time; pro inspection every 3 years or post-major-storm
- Brisbane storm season Oct–Mar — extra vigilance after big storms

### Electrical + RCD

- Push-button test RCDs quarterly (Mar / Jun / Sep / Dec)
- Pro electrical inspection every 5 yrs or before insurance renewal

### Plumbing + hot water

- Walk-around for leaks annually (Dec)
- Hot water service flush annually (July); anode rod check every 5 yrs (DIY or plumber)
- Pro plumbing inspection every 5 yrs for older homes

### HVAC

- Split-system filter clean quarterly (DIY — 10 min per unit)
- Annual pro service before high-use season (March in QLD)

### Gas

- Gas-stove check annually (QLD doesn't mandate annual certificates for owner-occupiers; rentals different)

### Pool / barrier (QLD)

- Weekly chemical test (DIY)
- Monthly pool service (pro $80/mo)
- **Pool fence certificate annually** — booking 4 weeks ahead in summer; QBCC-registered inspector

### Termite + pest

- Annual termite inspection (April) — non-negotiable in subtropical Brisbane
- Spot quarterly visual checks (skirting boards, garage frame)

### QLD-specific compliance

- Photoelectric interconnected smoke alarms — full compliance by 2027
- Pool barrier annual certification (Form 23) from licensed inspector
- Cyclone-prone glass treatment + storm shutters if north of Maryborough
