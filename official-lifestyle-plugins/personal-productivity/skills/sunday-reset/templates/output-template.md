## Express Version (12 minutes — low-energy weeks)

1. **Carryforward kill** (3 min) — what's been on the list 3+ weeks? Kill it now.
2. **Top-3 for next week** (3 min) — only three. Nothing else gets named.
3. **One high-stakes meeting prep** (3 min) — single decision + outcome.
4. **One reflection prompt** (3 min) — pick from library; write 2 sentences.

The express version still counts. A 12-minute week is better than a skipped week.

## Reflection Prompt Library

Rotate weekly. Do not use the same prompt twice in a month.

1. What went well that I didn't celebrate?
2. Where did I get in my own way?
3. What would I tell a friend in my position right now?
4. What did I commit to that I shouldn't have?
5. What's one thing I now know that I didn't last Sunday?
6. Who deserves a thank-you message I haven't sent?
7. What boundary did I keep this week that I'm proud of?
8. What pattern am I noticing across the last 3–4 weeks?
9. What am I avoiding by being busy?
10. If I removed one recurring thing from my calendar, what would it be?
11. What's the smallest improvement I could test next week?
12. What was true this week that won't be true forever?
