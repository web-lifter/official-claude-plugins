# Sunday Reset — Marcus (founder, 12-person SaaS, Brisbane)

**When:** Sunday 7:30pm
**Where:** Kitchen table — laptop closed, paper notebook, mug of tea
**Total time:** 55 minutes (full) | 12 minutes (express)

---

## Full Reset Ritual

| # | Step | Time | Notes |
|---|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Get clear — empty inboxes (email, Slack DMs, Notion captures, voice memos) | 10 min | Don't process — just dump into one capture list |
| 2 | Get current — calendar review (past week + next two) | 8 min | Cross-check investor follow-ups, hiring loops |
| 3 | Carryforward & kill — apply 3-tier rule | 5 min | Brutal; anything on the list 3+ weeks gets killed tonight |
| 4 | Founder scan — cash, fundraise, hiring, north star | 8 min | Even one sentence on each — refuses to be skipped |
| 5 | Get creative — brainstorm capture | 5 min | One column in the paper notebook; no judgement |
| 6 | Set intention — top 3 for next week | 7 min | Eisenhower-filter; Q4 items deleted, not deferred |
| 7 | Meeting & decision pre-load | 7 min | Only the 2 highest-stakes meetings get prep |
| 8 | One reflection prompt | 5 min | Rotate weekly from library |

---

## Express Version (12 minutes — for hung-over Sundays, sick kids, or post-incident weeks)

1. **Carryforward kill** (3 min) — anything 3+ weeks old gets killed.
2. **Top-3 for next week** (3 min) — only three. Nothing else gets named.
3. **One high-stakes meeting prep** (3 min) — single decision + outcome.
4. **One reflection prompt** (3 min) — two sentences, paper notebook.

A 12-minute reset still counts. A skipped reset is the failure mode.

## Carryforward & Kill Rules

| Reschedule count | Action |
|-----------------|--------|
| 1st | Reschedule with a date |
| 2nd | Demote to someday/maybe (Notion "later" board) |
| 3rd | Kill — write one-line reason in journal |

**Someday/maybe pruning:** Last Sunday of each quarter — 20 minutes.

**Non-negotiables (these move regardless):**

- Investor monthly update — last business day of every month
- Wife's birthday plan locked by 30/11 every year
- Annual board pack drafted by week 4 of each quarter

## Meeting & Decision Pre-load

| Meeting | Single decision | Two outcomes wanted | The killer question |
|---------|----------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Mon 10am — Head of Sales 1:1 | Whether to hire the second AE in May or July | (a) sign in May; (b) wait to July | What conversion-to-paid did we close at in April? |
| Wed 2pm — Bridge round investor call | Whether we open the round at $4M or $6M post | (a) $4M post, faster close; (b) $6M post, slower | What's the 12-month burn if we close at each? |

**Recurring decisions to eliminate this week:**

- Lunch on Tuesdays — always the same café, no decision needed
- Standup format — fix to written-async on M/W/F; no live meeting on those days

## Reflection Prompt Library

Rotate weekly. No prompt repeats within a month.

1. What went well that I didn't celebrate?
2. Where did I get in my own way?
3. What would I tell a friend in my position right now?
4. What did I commit to that I shouldn't have?
5. What's one thing I now know that I didn't last Sunday?
6. Who deserves a thank-you message I haven't sent?
7. What boundary did I keep this week that I'm proud of?
8. What pattern am I noticing across the last 3–4 weeks?
9. What am I avoiding by being busy?
10. If I removed one recurring thing from my calendar, what would it be?
11. What's the smallest improvement I could test next week?
12. What was true this week that won't be true forever?
