## Distraction Guardrails

| Layer | Rule |
|-------|------|
| **Device** | Phone in another room; Slack DND; email closed; only required browser tabs |
| **Calendar** | Day blocked as `(deep focus — no meetings)` weeks ahead; 1:1s moved off |
| **Comms** | One 15-min comms check between Block 3 and Block 4 — not earlier |
| **Environment** | Workspace prepped the night before; water + snack at desk; same playlist or silence |
| **Auto-reply** | See below — copy and paste into Slack DND |
| **Tapper script** | "Can it wait 90 minutes? I'm in a deep block." |

## Day-After Rule

The day after a deep-focus day is **lighter** by design.

- No new deep blocks. Admin, 1:1s, learning, walks.
- Recovery is part of the rhythm. Two heroic days back-to-back is the failure mode.
