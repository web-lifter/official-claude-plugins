## The Stack

1. **After my first cup of coffee, I will drink a full glass of water before opening Slack.**
2. **After each Calendar invite ends, I will stand and do 5 chest-opener stretches before sitting back down.**
3. **After lunch, I will walk outside for 10 minutes — sunlight + steps.**
4. **Before every meeting, I will take 5 slow box-breaths (4-in, 4-hold, 4-out, 4-hold).**
5. **At lights-out, I will take 3 long exhales and put my phone in the kitchen.**

## Failure-Mode Recovery

| Mode | Recovery move |
|------|---------------|
| Back-to-back meetings, no time for stretch | Do one chest-opener on the way to the bathroom |
| Travel — anchors absent | Keep #4 (breath) + #5 (lights-out exhale) — they're portable |
| Got the flu | Hydration only; resume the rest after 2 days of feeling normal |
