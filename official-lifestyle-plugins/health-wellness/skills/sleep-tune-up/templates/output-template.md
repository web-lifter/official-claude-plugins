## 14-Day Protocol

| Days | Focus | Daily actions |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1–3 | Light + routine | Morning light 10 min within 30 min of waking; caffeine cutoff 2pm; fixed lights-on time |
| 4–7 | Wind-down | 60-min wind-down: screens off, warm shower, dim lights, slow reading |
| 8–10 | Environment | Bedroom ≤19°C; blackout; phone in another room; white noise if partner snores |
| 11–14 | Stress / worry | Worry list 90 min before bed; 4-7-8 breathing at lights-out if mind racing |

**Bookend rules:**
- Same wake-time ±15 min, including weekends
- Lights-out ±30 min nightly
- No alcohol within 3h of bed for the full 14 days

## Caffeine + Alcohol + Screen Rules

- **Caffeine** — last serve by 2pm. If sensitive, by 12pm. Includes tea (smaller dose but matters).
- **Alcohol** — none within 3h of bed; max 2 standard drinks on weekends.
- **Screens** — off 60 min before lights-out. Phone outside the bedroom.
