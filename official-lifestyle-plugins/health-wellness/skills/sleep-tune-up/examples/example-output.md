## Dominant Lever

**Wind-down + caffeine timing.** Jen is having 2pm flat whites + checking emails until 11pm, then struggling to fall asleep and waking at 3am with a racing mind.

Why: caffeine half-life is ~5h, so a 2pm flat white still has ~70mg active at 7pm and ~35mg at midnight. Late-evening email triggers mental arousal in the wind-down window.
