## Program at a Glance

- **Goal:** Hybrid — keep getting stronger while running 2× per week
- **Length:** 12 weeks (3 blocks × 4 weeks, deload every 4th week)
- **Sessions per week:** 4 (2 strength + 2 run)
- **Equipment tier:** Home gym (rack + barbell + plates + DBs)
- **Estimated session time:** Strength 60 min; runs 45–60 min

## Progression Rules

- **Squat / bench / row / RDL** — +2.5kg next session when all reps × sets completed clean (RIR ≥ 1). Stall twice → deload that lift only.
- **Chin-ups** — add reps before adding weight. When 3×10 clean → add a 2.5kg dip belt.
- **Z2 run** — pace climbs naturally as HR settles; do not chase pace.
- **Threshold intervals** — add 30 seconds to the work interval every 2 weeks.

## Recovery Cues

- **Sleep:** target 7.5h; below 6.5h scale volume −20%.
- **Soreness vs pain:** sore quads after Thu squats fine; sharp knee pain mid-squat → stop, see physio.
- **Active recovery:** Wed and Sat walks are part of the program — not optional.
- **Nutrition:** 30g protein within 90 min post-session; carb-load Thu lunch before squat day.
