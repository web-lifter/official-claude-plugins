## Progression Rules

- **Strength lifts** — +2.5kg next session when all reps × sets completed with RIR ≥ 1.
- **Hypertrophy lifts** — double progression: hit the top of the rep range across all sets → add load.
- **Endurance** — heart-rate Z2 stays steady week-to-week; threshold work progresses by 30s/wk on intervals.

## Recovery Cues

- **Sleep:** ≥ 7h. If consistently < 6.5h, scale volume −20%.
- **Soreness vs pain:** 24–72h muscle soreness is fine. Sharp pain that changes movement → see clinician.
- **Active recovery day:** walk 20–30 min outside; no structured cardio.
- **Nutrition:** ~30g protein within 90 min post-session. Link to `[[week-of-meals]]`.
