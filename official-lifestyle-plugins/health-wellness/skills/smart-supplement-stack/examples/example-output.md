## Food-First Checklist

Address these first; they cover most "supplement gaps":

- **Oily fish 2× per week** (salmon, sardines, mackerel) — could replace fish oil if consistent
- **Sunlight 10–15 min per day** before 10am in shorts/sleeves — covers vitamin D in summer
- **Red meat 1–2× per week** — covers iron, B12, zinc, creatine partially
- **Diverse vegetables daily** — covers most micronutrients the multivitamin tries to fill
