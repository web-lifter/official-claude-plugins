## Prep-Day Routines

### Sunday afternoon (3 hours total)

**Best for:** weekday-focused workers who eat out Saturday.

- Cook longest-time items first (roast, slow-cook protein, baked potatoes/sweet potatoes)
- Cook one big pot of rice or grain (4+ servings)
- Brown one mince / stir-fry one protein
- Chop salad veg, portion snacks
- Boil 6+ eggs

### Saturday morning (2 hours)

**Best for:** parents who want Sunday free with family.

- Same as Sunday plan, but split shopping → cook into two blocks.

### Mid-week split (2 × 45 min — Sun PM + Wed PM)

**Best for:** if 5-day-old protein doesn't sit well.

- Sunday: Mon/Tue/Wed lunches and dinners.
- Wednesday: Thu/Fri lunches and dinners.

## Macro-Target Quick Reference Tables

Assumes Mifflin–St Jeor TDEE estimation. **Always verify against actual body composition over 2–3 weeks.**

### Maintenance (sedentary office worker, light gym 2×/week)

| Body mass | Calories | Protein | Fat | Carbs |
|-----------|----------|---------|-----|-------|
| 60kg F | 1,800 | 100g | 65g | 200g |
| 70kg F | 2,000 | 120g | 70g | 220g |
| 75kg M | 2,200 | 130g | 75g | 250g |
| 80kg M | 2,400 | 140g | 80g | 280g |
| 90kg M | 2,600 | 150g | 90g | 290g |

### Recomp (slight surplus or maintenance + heavy training 3–5×/week)

Add 200–300 kcal to maintenance; bump protein to 1.8–2.2 g/kg.

### Weight loss (sustainable 0.4–0.7 kg/wk)

Subtract 300–500 kcal from maintenance; protein stays high (1.8–2.2 g/kg); fat 0.8 g/kg; carbs fill remainder. **No aggressive cuts (>500 kcal deficit) without supervision.**

### Hypertrophy bulk

Add 300–500 kcal to maintenance; protein 1.8–2.2 g/kg; carbs prioritised around training.
