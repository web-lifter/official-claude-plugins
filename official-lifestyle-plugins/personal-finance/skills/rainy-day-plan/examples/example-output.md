## Playbooks

### 1. Layoff / Redundancy (Lucas)

1. **Day 0:** Don't sign anything offered on the spot. Take it home. Read it overnight.
2. **Day 1:** Calculate redundancy entitlement (Fair Work calculator). Note final pay date.
3. **Day 2:** Apply for JobSeeker on Services Australia (1-week waiting period; can backdate to day after job ends).
4. **Day 5:** Review weekly expenses; cancel non-essential subscriptions; switch to "lean budget".
5. **Day 7:** Contact mortgage lender's hardship team; request hardship variation if needed.
6. **Day 14:** Update LinkedIn; reach out to 5 specific people (not generic).
7. **Day 30:** Review buffer runway; if extending past 90 days, refinance / consolidate any debts.
8. **Day 90:** Consider super early-release on financial hardship grounds (last resort; ATO has criteria).

### 2. Illness / Disability (medium-term)

1. **Day 1:** Notify employer; understand sick-leave + LSL entitlements.
2. **Day 3:** Contact income protection insurer (if cover in place) — lodge claim.
3. **Day 7:** Apply for Centrelink Sickness Allowance + check Medicare safety net.
4. **Day 30:** Activate ATO hardship if tax owing.
5. **Day 60:** Review mortgage; lender hardship if needed.
6. **Day 90+:** Consider TPD claim if condition is permanent.

### 3. Family Emergency (interstate / urgent care)

1. Lucas → take compassionate leave / annual leave / carer's leave (Fair Work).
2. Hannah → travel only if essential; childcare network mobilised.
3. Buffer use: up to $5,000 from buffer for emergency travel; replenish on return.
4. Communication tree: 1 person in each side of family is the "info hub".
5. ATO / lenders: only contact if absence > 4 weeks.

### 4. Separation / Relationship Breakdown

1. **First call:** A community legal centre (free) — listings at nationallegalaid.org or LawAccess.
2. **Banking:** Move half of joint funds to a sole-name account at a different bank (legal step in most circumstances; confirm with legal).
3. **Update:** super beneficiary, life-insurance beneficiary, will.
4. **Children:** keep routines stable; document arrangements.
5. **Support:** Beyond Blue 1300 22 4636 / Lifeline 13 11 14 / Relationships Australia 1300 364 277.

### 5. Natural Disaster

1. **Immediate:** Safety first. Evacuate if instructed.
2. **Day 1:** Document all damage with photo + video before any cleanup.
3. **Day 2:** Lodge insurance claim by phone (faster than online).
4. **Day 7:** Apply for Disaster Recovery Payment / Allowance via Services Australia.
5. **Day 14:** Lender hardship; rates / utility holds.
6. **Day 30+:** Tax: replacement of destroyed records via ATO; donations claim eligible.
