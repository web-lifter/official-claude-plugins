## Playbooks

### 1. Injury / illness — can't work for 4–12 weeks

1. **Day 1:** GP — document the injury for insurance + worker recovery later
2. **Day 2:** Lodge income protection claim (90-day wait period applies)
3. **Day 5:** Notify active jobs — refer or reschedule
4. **Day 14:** Apply for Sickness Allowance (now under JobSeeker) via Services Australia for the wait-period gap
5. **Day 30:** ATO hardship if BAS / PAYG instalments owing
6. **Day 60:** Mortgage / rent hardship if needed
7. **Day 90:** Income protection payments commence (typical)
8. **Recovery:** physio + cleared-to-work assessment from treating GP before resuming

### 2. Major equipment loss (van fire, theft, write-off)

1. **Hour 1:** Report to police (theft) or document scene (fire) for insurance
2. **Hour 2:** Notify tools + vehicle insurer; start the claim
3. **Day 1:** Identify a temporary ute (hire-purchase via Get Loaded or similar trades-specific) + cover for tools
4. **Day 3:** Notify active jobs — delay 5–10 working days
5. **Week 2:** Replacement claim paid (typically) — restock essentials only initially
6. **Month 2:** Full restock + lessons-learned audit of insurance cover adequacy

### 3. Tax/BAS surprise (unexpected ATO bill)

1. **Hour 1:** Call ATO Business line 13 28 66 — request payment plan
2. **Day 1:** Engage accountant for review; rarely is the first ATO calc the final one
3. **Day 7:** If payment plan agreed, set up direct-debit
4. **Quarter end:** Review tax-account setaside — increase % if undershooting

### 4. Slow quarter (June + early July traditionally lean for trades)

1. **Month -1 (May):** Confirm buffer covers expected 6-week lean period
2. **Month 0:** Reduce discretionary spending; defer non-urgent equipment purchases
3. **Month +1:** Activate quoted-but-not-booked work; offer 7-day-quick-pay discount
4. **Month +2:** If extending past 8 weeks, contact mortgage/lender hardship preemptively

### 5. Apprentice hire (workers comp + payroll obligations)

1. **Step 1:** Workers comp policy — mandatory before first day on site
2. **Step 2:** Payroll tax check — only applicable if total wages > state threshold
3. **Step 3:** Super for apprentice — 12% SG (rising) into chosen fund
4. **Step 4:** Single Touch Payroll registration with ATO
5. **Step 5:** Apprenticeship training contract via VET regulator
