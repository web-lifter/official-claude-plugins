## Strategic Summary

**Where pattersonwebb.com.au leads:**
- Neither competitor has meaningfully developed SMSF or investment property content — potential first-mover opportunity with high-value client intent.
- Patterson & Webb has a cleaner, more professional visual presentation (qualitative observation) that could support better conversion once traffic improves.

**Where pattersonwebb.com.au trails:**
- 42 indexed pages vs 187 (Hills) and 94 (Numbers Co) — content gap is severe. Both competitors have 3–5x more indexed content.
- Authority score (14) is significantly behind Hills Accounting (38) — link acquisition is a multi-year project; content volume is addressable in months.
- Neither Hills nor Numbers Co has FAQ schema on all pages; Patterson & Webb has zero FAQ schema — immediate fix available.
- Hills Accounting's internal linking structure (service page ↔ guide hub) is a topical authority signal Patterson & Webb is not exploiting.

**Top 3 highest-leverage competitive actions:**
1. **Create a dedicated `/tax-return-sydney` service page** — highest-volume transactional gap (1,900/month core keyword, 4,800 cluster volume). Both competitors rank in top 10; Patterson & Webb has no page. Format: 1,000-word service page with FAQPage schema, pricing/timeline information, and a clear CTA. Estimated time to top-10: 3–6 months with 5 relevant local links.
2. **Add FAQ schema to all existing service pages and produce 3–5 guide articles** targeting the "work from home tax deductions", "BAS lodgement", and "Xero accountant" clusters — these are informational and commercial queries where Hills Accounting earns significant traffic via guide content. Patterson & Webb has no guides.
3. **Pursue 20–30 additional referring domains** through local business directories (BNI, local Chambers of Commerce), industry bodies (CPA Australia member directory), and client testimonial placements — Hills Accounting's 94 referring domains are the primary authority driver separating the two sites. Close the gap to 50 domains within 12 months.
