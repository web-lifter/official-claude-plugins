## 12. Acceptance Criteria

The logo is accepted when:
- [ ] It works at the smallest specified reproduction size (favicon test passed)
- [ ] It works in pure black on white
- [ ] It works in pure white on the brand primary
- [ ] It is vector-source-clean (no rasters, no embedded effects)
- [ ] It includes all deliverables in the checklist
- [ ] It avoids every item in the avoid list
- [ ] The designer can articulate the conceptual rationale in one paragraph
