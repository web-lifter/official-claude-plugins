## 6. Refund / Returns Policy (DRAFT — REVIEW REQUIRED)

**Harborline Returns Policy**

**Last updated:** 11/04/2026

### Australian Consumer Law

Our products come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. If a product is faulty, not of acceptable quality, not fit for purpose, or doesn't match its description, you are entitled to a remedy — refund, replacement, or repair, depending on the nature of the issue.

For major failures (e.g. a jacket that completely fails its waterproofing on the first wear), you can choose between a refund and a replacement. For minor failures, we may choose between repairing the product, replacing it, or refunding you.

### Our policy (in addition to your ACL rights)

In addition to your statutory rights under the Australian Consumer Law, Harborline offers:

- **30-day "didn't fit" return:** Unworn items in original condition can be returned within 30 days of receipt for a full refund (excluding original shipping costs).
- **365-day workmanship guarantee:** If a stitch fails, a zip breaks, or any workmanship issue arises within 365 days, contact us for a free repair or replacement.

### How to request a return

1. Email returns@harborline.com.au with your order number and a brief explanation
2. We'll respond within 2 business days with return instructions
3. Pack the item and post it to the return address we provide
4. We refund within 5 business days of receiving the returned item

### What's not eligible for "didn't fit" return

- Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
- Items that are missing tags
- Items returned more than 30 days after delivery
- Custom or personalised items (none currently sold)

### Original shipping

Original shipping costs are not refunded for "didn't fit" returns. Return shipping is paid by the customer for "didn't fit" returns. For ACL-related returns (faults, not as described), we cover all shipping.

### Contact

For returns questions: returns@harborline.com.au
