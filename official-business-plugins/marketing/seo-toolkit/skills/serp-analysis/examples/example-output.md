## Strategic Recommendation

The "best CRM for tradies Australia" SERP is dominated by software aggregators (Capterra, GetApp, SoftwareAdvice) producing templated listicles. Their weakness: generic, US-skewed content with pricing in USD and no AU-specific workflow knowledge.

A purpose-built, Australia-first comparison article targeting this query should:
1. Lead with AUD pricing (inclusive of GST) for all 6–8 tools covered.
2. Include sections for specific trades verticals (electricians, plumbers, builders, HVAC).
3. Embed a comparison table at the top — this format is most likely to earn a Featured Snippet or AI Overview citation.
4. Answer all 4 PAA questions as explicit H2 sub-sections with concise direct answers.
5. Include a "what most tradies actually need" section (mirrors Tradify's approach at position 8, which is the only brand result in top 10 — indicates Google accepts vendor content in this cluster).
