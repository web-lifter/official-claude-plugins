1. **QR code at counter:** Print a QR code linking directly to the GBP review page and place it at the payment point. "Enjoyed your coffee? Share a review — it takes 30 seconds." Expected velocity increase: 4–8 reviews/month from this action alone.

4. **Avoid review gating.** Do not pre-screen customers — ask all customers, not just those you think had a positive experience. This is required by Google's guidelines and Australian Consumer Law.
