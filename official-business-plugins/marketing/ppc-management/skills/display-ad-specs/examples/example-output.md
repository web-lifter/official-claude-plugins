## Designer brief

### Brand context

Koala & Co. is a small-batch Australian homewares maker. The visual language is understated, natural, and quietly confident — think early morning light on natural fibres, rumpled linen, hand-worked wool in earthy palettes. The brand avoids hype, exclamation marks, and retail-bright aesthetics. Instead it leans into texture, calm composition, and negative space.

### Composition

Hero product centred or slightly off-centre in each image, shot on a styled bedroom or living room set. Natural morning light (no harsh studio lighting). Background should be warm neutral — oatmeal linen, untreated timber, natural stone. Brand logo small in the bottom right, never centre. No text overlay on most images; where text is used, it sits in the bottom third with 40–60 px safe zone and uses the brand's serif display face at 28–36 px.

### Do-not list

- No lifestyle shots with people's faces (stock-photo vibe).
- No neon, high-saturation, or synthetic backgrounds.
- No photos with visible packaging or price tags.
- No text overlay longer than 4 words.
- No cluttered product groupings — one or two items per image maximum.
- No competitors' visual language (specifically avoid the bright, product-on-white-background look of Adairs or Bed Bath N Table).

### Production notes

- Shoot list: 25 total images — 10 of throws, 8 of rugs, 7 of cushions. Each product shot twice (hero + lifestyle).
- Delivery format: JPG, sRGB colour profile, quality 85–90, ≤5 MB each.
- File naming: `koala_pmax-homewares-q2_{aspect}_{##}.jpg` — e.g. `koala_pmax-homewares-q2_square_01.jpg`.
- Deadline: Friday 26 April 2026 17:00 AEST.
- Video deadline: Friday 3 May 2026 — 30 sec hero video, slow pans across hero shots, minimal text, soft instrumental soundtrack (licensed via Artlist or equivalent).
