# Meta Ads Copy — Reference

## 1. Character limits (2026)

| Asset | Mobile cutoff | Max |
|---|---|---|
| Primary text | 125 chars before "See more" | ~500 usable, no hard max |
| Headline | 27 | 40 (desktop only) |
| Description | 27 | 30 (desktop only) |
| Link URL display | — | — |
| CTA button | pre-set list | — |

## 2. CTA button options by objective

| Objective | Primary CTAs | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| OUTCOME_SALES | Shop Now, Order Now, Buy Now | Shop Now is the default. |
| OUTCOME_LEADS | Sign Up, Contact Us, Get Quote, Book Now, Subscribe, Apply Now | Pick by the lead intent. |
| OUTCOME_TRAFFIC | Learn More, See Menu, Listen Now, Watch More | Default Learn More. |
| OUTCOME_APP_PROMOTION | Download, Install Now, Play Game | Play Game for games only. |
| OUTCOME_AWARENESS | Learn More | Almost always. |
| OUTCOME_ENGAGEMENT | Follow, Like Page, View Offer | Pick by engagement type. |

## 3. Angle library

For every copy pack, rotate through these angles:

### Problem-focused
"Every Australian winter, the same story: cold couches, thin blankets, synthetic throws that pill after a month. Koala & Co. makes throws that don't." (157 chars)

### Benefit-focused
"A handmade merino throw that gets softer with every wash, made in small batches in Australia. Free shipping over $150." (116 chars)

### Story / UGC
"I bought my first Koala throw in 2023. It's still on the couch. Three winters, no pilling, no fading. Here's the story." (118 chars)

### Social proof
"10,000 Australian homes have chosen Koala throws. Here's why they don't go back." (77 chars)

### Urgency / offer
"Winter throws, $129. Made in Australia. Free shipping until Sunday. Limited stock — new batch in 6 weeks." (106 chars)

### Contrarian / polarising
"I used to think wool throws were a waste of money. Then I spent a winter under one. Here's what changed." (104 chars)

## 5. Emoji dictionary

| Emoji | Usage |
|---|---|
| 📦 | Shipping, delivery, package |
| ✨ | New, sparkle, launch |
| 🔥 | Popular, trending, sale |
| ⭐ | Reviews, ratings |
| 📍 | Location, geography |
| 💬 | Support, chat, conversation |
| 🇦🇺 | Australian-made (use sparingly) |
| 🌿 | Natural, sustainable |

Use at most **2 per copy block**. Avoid strings of emoji.

## 6. Copy anti-patterns

- **ALL CAPS.** "AMAZING DEALS!!!"
- **Exclamation stacking.** "Don't miss out!!!"
- **Superlatives without proof.** "The best throw in Australia"
- **Corporate voice.** "Our team of experts crafts premium products"
- **Emoji explosion.** "🔥🔥🔥 SALE! 🎉🎉🎉"
- **Fake urgency.** "Only 3 left!" when there are 300
- **Competitor name drops.** "Unlike Adairs..." (Meta may disapprove)
- **Clickbait.** "You won't believe..."
