## Objection handling

### "$129 feels expensive for a throw"

It is. But mass-produced throws cost $40–$60 and last a winter. A Koala throw lasts 5+ winters of daily use, which works out to ~$26 per winter. Plus you'll actually use it — it doesn't end up at the op shop in 12 months.

### "How is this different from an IKEA throw?"

Three things: (1) merino wool, not synthetic — warmer and breathable. (2) handmade, so the weave is tighter. (3) made in Marrickville by people you can email, not in a factory halfway around the world. Try one. If it's not different, return it.

### "I'm not sure about the colour on my couch"

We include fabric swatches in every order so you can see it in your space. If the colour doesn't work, the full return policy applies. No stress.

### "Shipping to my regional area"

We ship anywhere in Australia. Metro areas: 2–3 days. Regional: 4–7 days. Free over $150. Express available at checkout.

## FAQ

**Q:** Are the throws machine washable?

**A:** Yes — cold machine wash, gentle cycle. Lay flat to dry. Full care instructions are included with every order.

**Q:** Where is the wool sourced from?

**A:** We use Australian Responsible Wool Standard certified merino from farms in NSW and Victoria.

**Q:** Can I see the colours before I buy?

**A:** Every order includes a fabric swatch card. If the colour doesn't work in your space, return the throw (30-day policy).

**Q:** Do you ship internationally?

**A:** Yes — available at checkout. International orders typically take 5–15 business days.

**Q:** Can I wash multiple throws at once?

**A:** We recommend washing one at a time on gentle cycle — the wool is dense and overloading the machine strains the fibres.
