## Production spec

- **Reel:** 1080×1920, MP4 H.264, ≤4 GB, 18 seconds, phone-shot acceptable
- **Feed image:** 1080×1350 (4:5), JPG ≤85% quality, ≤5 MB
- **Carousel (5 cards):** 1080×1080 (1:1) each, JPG ≤5 MB each
- **Captions:** Burned-in on Reel (no SRT), white text with 4 px black stroke, bottom third, within safe zone
- **Music:** Licensed from Artlist, soft acoustic guitar, instrumental only, no vocals
- **Brand asset usage:** Koala & Co. logo in final 2 seconds of Reel, bottom right of static assets

---

## Brand and voice constraints

Voice from `brand-manager:brand-identity` — warm, direct, quietly confident, AU English, no hype, no exclamation marks. Avoid superlatives. Lean into specificity (prices, materials, geographic origin). Avoid corporate / stock-photo aesthetic. Do not compare directly to competitors by name. The throw is the hero, not the human model.
