# Meta Creative Brief — Reference

## 1. Meta ad format matrix (2026)

| Format | Aspect | Dimensions | Duration | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reels video | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | 9–90s (≥15 recommended) | Prospecting, top-funnel |
| Story | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | 1–15s | Quick awareness, retargeting |
| Feed video (square) | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | 1–15s | Retargeting, versatile |
| Feed video (vertical) | 4:5 | 1080×1350 | 1–15s | Mobile feed, mid-funnel |
| Feed single image (square) | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | — | Retargeting, quick tests |
| Feed single image (vertical) | 4:5 | 1080×1350 | — | Mobile feed |
| Feed single image (landscape) | 1.91:1 | 1200×628 | — | Desktop feed (minor usage) |
| Carousel (2–10 cards) | 1:1 per card | 1080×1080 | — per card | Multi-product / multi-benefit |
| Collection ad | hero image/video + 4 product tiles | Mixed | — | Retail catalogues |

## 2. Safe zones

Mobile UI chrome eats the edges. Keep text well inside:

- **Reels/Story (9:16):** 250 px top (header), 350 px bottom (captions + CTA). Safe text zone = 1080×1320 centred.
- **Feed 1:1:** 60 px each side.
- **Feed 4:5:** 60 px top/bottom, 60 px sides.

## 3. Copy limits

| Asset | Max chars (feed) | Max chars (reels) |
|---|---|---|
| Primary text | 125 chars before "... See more" | ~125 |
| Headline | 27 | ~40 (less prominent) |
| Description | 27 | — |

## 4. CTA button options

- Shop Now (e-commerce)
- Sign Up (sign-up flows)
- Learn More (content + awareness)
- Book Now (service bookings)
- Contact Us (lead-gen)
- Get Offer (coupon / promo)
- Subscribe (newsletter)
- Download (apps)
- Order Now (food / delivery)
- Request Time (appointments)

## 5. Hook formulas (proven patterns)

- **Question:** "What if [status quo] could change in [timeframe]?"
- **Stat:** "[Number] [target audience] [action] [result]"
- **Polarising:** "I used to [old belief], then I [change]"
- **Demo:** "Watch this [product] [demonstrate unique benefit]"
- **UGC/POV:** "POV: [relatable moment about the product]"
- **Before/After:** "[Before state] → [After state]"
- **Story:** "[Relatable character] with [problem] [discovers solution]"
- **Agitate:** "[Pain point]. Here's why nobody talks about it."

## 6. Production principles

- **Hook in frame 1.** Not frame 5.
- **Mute-first.** Captions always on. Sound is a bonus.
- **Text overlay ≤ 3 words per moment.**
- **Motion every 2–3 seconds.** Cuts, zooms, pans, text animations. Meta viewers scroll fast.
- **Brand appears in the first 3 seconds AND the last 3 seconds.**
- **Resolution: 1080p minimum.** 4K is unnecessary.

## 7. Anti-patterns

- **15-second product-only shots.** Borderline unwatchable.
- **Script read with corporate voiceover.** Feels dated on Meta.
- **No captions.** ~85% of Meta video is watched without sound.
- **Dense text overlays.** Meta algorithm may down-rank.
- **Competitor mentions** — blocked by Meta.
- **"Amazing", "Best", "#1"** — generic + may trigger disapproval.

## 8. UGC-style production

UGC (user-generated content) ads have consistently outperformed polished ads since 2021 — they feel native, less ad-like, more trustworthy.

- Phone camera, natural light, no crew.
- Person holds or uses the product.
- Speaks to camera like a video message.
- Reference price, delivery, or specific experience.
- 15–30 seconds.
- Ends with "Shop [brand]" + URL.

Phones are fine. Polished is not required. Authenticity > polish.
