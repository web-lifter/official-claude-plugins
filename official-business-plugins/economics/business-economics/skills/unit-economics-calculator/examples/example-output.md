## 7. Strategic Narrative and Action Items

### What the numbers say

Spark Digital has **strong unit economics** with an LTV:CAC ratio nearly 9x and a payback period under 3 months. The business generates cash quickly from new clients and retains them for an average of 32 months. The 68% gross margin is at the top of the Australian agency benchmark range.

### Where the risk lives

1. **Churn sensitivity.** A 2-percentage-point increase in monthly churn reduces LTV by 40%. The business is one bad quarter away from the downside scenario.
2. **Labour cost exposure.** Direct labour is 22.5% of revenue. Senior digital strategist salaries in Brisbane have risen 18% in 24 months. Each 5pp margin compression costs $153K annually at current scale.
3. **Channel concentration.** Referrals (25%) and content/SEO (23%) are the lowest-CAC channels but take 6-12 months to scale. Paid channels have 2-4x higher CAC.

### Recommended actions

| # | Action | Expected Impact | Effort |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Introduce 12-month contracts with 5% discount vs month-to-month | Reduce churn from 3.1% to ~2.2%, adding $8.4K LTV per client | Medium |
| 2 | Launch tiered pricing with $4,800/mo Premium tier including analytics dashboards | Increase blended ACV by 12-15%, improve contribution margin | High |
| 3 | Formalise referral program with $500 credit incentive per qualified introduction | Scale lowest-CAC channel ($708) from 25% to 35% of new business | Low |
| 4 | Hire junior strategists and implement senior-to-junior leverage model (1:3 ratio) | Protect gross margin against rising senior salaries, save ~$96K/yr | High |
| 5 | Implement client health scoring (NPS + engagement metrics) with automated alerts | Early intervention on at-risk accounts, target 15% reduction in churn | Medium |
