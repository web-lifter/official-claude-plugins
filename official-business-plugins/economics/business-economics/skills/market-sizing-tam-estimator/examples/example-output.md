---

## 9. Key Takeaways

1. **Large underpenetrated market.** With incumbents capturing under 9% of TAM, the opportunity is in converting paper/spreadsheet users rather than displacing competitors.
2. **ACV and adoption rate are the highest-sensitivity variables.** Pricing strategy and onboarding friction directly control SOM outcomes.
3. **$18.6M Year 3 SOM is conservative.** Assumes no expansion into adjacent verticals (landscaping, cleaning) or geographic markets (NZ, UK).
4. **Recommended validation:** Survey 200 trade businesses on current tooling and willingness-to-pay to refine assumption A2 (SaaS adoption willingness) before go-to-market spend.
