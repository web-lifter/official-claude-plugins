## Recommendations

1. **Negotiate COGS at next supplier review** — minimum order quantity at 5,000+ units unlocks ~10% rebate on ingredients + packaging. Current 2,800/mo is in the worst MOQ band.
2. **Plan in-house pick-pack at 7,500 units/mo** — one-off $40k saves $1.50/unit indefinitely; payback at 27k cumulative units (~4 months at 7,500/mo).
3. **Push for break-even by 4,200 units/mo at current pricing** — see `[[break-even-scenario-modeller]]` for the full scenario sensitivity. Without volume + COGS improvement, this business loses ~$33k/mo.
4. **Re-examine marketing ROAS** — at 25% of revenue, marketing is the largest variable cost; a 10% reduction (through better channel mix per `[[revenue-channel-mapper]]`) recovers $11,400/mo.
