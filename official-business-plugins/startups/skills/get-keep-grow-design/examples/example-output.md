# Get / Keep / Grow

Segment: in-house counsel at AU/NZ mid-market companies (50–500 staff). Procurement is the secondary segment — addressed by a separate get-keep-grow once procurement-led pilots begin.

## Get

| Activity | Metric | Cost / customer | Driver hypothesis |
|----------|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Founder-led LinkedIn outbound (Priya → primary segment) | Discovery-call bookings per week | AU$0 marginal (founder time); ~AU$600 fully-loaded | H-201 |
| ALPMA conference + state-branch events | Qualified discovery convos per event | AU$2,800 ÷ 5 convos ≈ AU$560 | H-101 |
| GC peer-network referrals (from paying customers) | Warm intros received per paying customer per 90 days | ~AU$0 | H-102 |
| Founder thought-leadership on LinkedIn (weekly post on AU contract-risk patterns) | Profile views / DMs / inbound discovery | AU$0 marginal | H-204 (new) |

## Keep

| Activity | Metric | Target | Driver hypothesis |
|----------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Weekly check-in with each pilot user (first 60 days) | Active-week ratio (weeks they reviewed ≥1 contract) | ≥ 75% | H-203 |
| Monthly "what we learned" digest (segment-wide newsletter) | Open rate; reply rate | ≥ 35% open, ≥ 5% reply | H-301 (new) |
| In-app obligation-deadline alerts (when contracts have date triggers) | Per-customer alert engagement | ≥ 1 acknowledged alert per month per active customer | H-302 (new) |
| Quarterly Business Review with each paying customer | NPS at QBR; renewal commit | NPS ≥ 30; ≥ 90% renewal | H-203 |

**Churn target:** ≤ 6% monthly (anchor for [churn-model.md](churn-model.md)). The Keep activities are designed to defend that rate.

## Grow

| Activity | Metric | Target | Driver hypothesis |
|----------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Seat expansion (add second / third lawyer when team grows) | Average seats per logo | ≥ 1.3 by month 9; ≥ 1.8 by month 18 | H-401 (new) |
| Cross-sell into procurement workflow (secondary segment) | Procurement-seat attach rate | ≥ 20% of paying logos by month 12 | H-402 (new) |
| Customer-led referrals — formalised "intro a peer, get a free seat for 3 months" programme | Referrals per paying customer per quarter | ≥ 1 in 2 customers refer at least once in first 6 months | H-102 |
| Annual contract uplift (month-to-month → annual, with 10% discount) | % annual-plan share | ≥ 40% on annual by month 18 | H-403 (new) |

## Notes

- Grow activities are intentionally hypothetical at this stage — ContractIQ is pre-revenue. Per Osterwalder & Pigneur (2010), the Customer Relationships cell is the right place to forecast Grow even before the venture has a single paying customer, so we don't lose the planning thread.
- Each Get activity feeds the funnel ([funnel-model.md](funnel-model.md)). Each Keep activity defends the [churn-model.md](churn-model.md) anchor rate.
