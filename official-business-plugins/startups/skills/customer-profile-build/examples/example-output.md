# Customer profile — AU/NZ mid-market in-house counsel

Right half of the VPC. Left half (products & services, pain relievers, gain creators) is built later by [`/value-map-build au-midmarket-inhouse-counsel`](../../../03-value-proposition/).

## Jobs

| ID | Job | Type | Priority |
|----|-----|------|----------|
| J-01 | Review a commercial MSA and produce a redline the business can sign | functional | high |
| J-02 | Flag risky clauses (uncapped liability, indemnity, auto-renewal, governing law) before signature | functional | high |
| J-03 | Track every "buyer shall…" obligation a signed contract creates | functional | high |
| J-04 | Defend a clause decision under audit or post-incident review | functional | medium |
| J-05 | Be seen by the business as a "fast" lawyer, not a bottleneck | social | high |
| J-06 | Feel confident they didn't miss a landmine | emotional | high |
| J-07 | Delegate junior reviews without losing oversight | functional | medium |

## Pains

| ID | Pain | Type | Priority |
|----|------|------|----------|
| P-01 | 3+ hours reviewing a 40-page MSA at 9pm because the business needs Friday sign-off | functional | high |
| P-02 | Missed an auto-renewal clause that auto-billed the company AU$50k–AU$200k 12–18 months later | functional | high |
| P-03 | No system tracks obligations once signed — relies on personal Excel sheets | functional | high |
| P-04 | Generic legal-AI tools (e.g. ChatGPT) raise confidentiality concerns | functional | high |
| P-05 | US-corpus AI tools miss AU/NZ-specific issues (PPSA, Privacy Act 1988 schedule 1, Modern Slavery Act 2018) | functional | medium |
| P-06 | Anxiety about being the single point of failure for legal review | emotional | high |
| P-07 | Embarrassment when finance discovers a missed clause months later | social | medium |

## Gains

| ID | Gain | Type | Priority |
|----|------|------|----------|
| G-01 | Redline a 40-page MSA in ≤ 20 minutes | required | high |
| G-02 | Plain-English explanation of each flagged clause for the business owner | required | high |
| G-03 | AU/NZ-trained corpus (PPSA, Privacy Act, Modern Slavery) | expected | high |
| G-04 | One-click obligation tracker auto-populated post-signature | desired | high |
| G-05 | Audit-defensible record of every accept/reject decision | expected | medium |
| G-06 | Precedent lookup ("what did we accept last quarter?") | desired | medium |
| G-07 | Confidence to delegate first-pass review to a paralegal or junior | desired | medium |
