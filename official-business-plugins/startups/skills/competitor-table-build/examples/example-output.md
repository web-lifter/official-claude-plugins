## Notes

- **Top 3 to target with full SWOT:** Spellbook, LawGeex, LinkSquares.
  Run `/swot-build` for each. Spellbook is highest priority (closest
  category match; reviewers will compare us to it directly).
- **Workaround dominance:** ~5 of 6 interviewees default to manual
  Word redlining. The substitute battle is against ChatGPT (informal,
  not-quite-allowed), not against the named CLM vendors.
- **Do-nothing severity:** the most expensive "competitor" — every
  missed auto-renewal, indemnity, or termination-for-convenience
  clause is a quantifiable loss. ContractIQ's pitch lands hardest on
  the team that has already paid this cost once.
