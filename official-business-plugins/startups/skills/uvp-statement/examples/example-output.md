# UVP

> For **in-house counsel at AU/NZ mid-market companies who review
> 8+ contracts a week**, our **ContractIQ** is a **contract-review
> co-pilot** that **finds risky clauses and missing protections in
> under 20 minutes**. Unlike **Spellbook**, we **are trained on AU/NZ
> commercial MSAs and flag jurisdiction-specific issues (PPSA,
> Privacy Act 1988 schedule 1, Modern Slavery Act 2018)**.
